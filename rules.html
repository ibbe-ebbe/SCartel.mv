Rules & Guidelines

1. Membership & Access

  • • Access to the gym is allowed only during official operating hours.
  • • Members must be at least 14 years old to join.
  • • Members under 18 must have the agreement completed and submitted by a parent or legal guardian.
  • • The parent or guardian submitting the form agrees to all gym rules on behalf of the underage member.
  • • Only registered members or authorized guardians are allowed on gym premises. No one else may enter, even if accompanying a member.
  • • The gym can suspend or terminate memberships for breaking the rules or misconduct.
  • • 24-hour CCTV is in place for safety. Footage is shared only with legal authorities if requested.
  • • The gym is not responsible for conflicts between members.

2. Health & Safety

  • • Members confirm they are healthy and can safely exercise.
  • • All members must use equipment correctly and return weights after use.
  • • Breaking or misusing equipment may result in paying for repairs and possible membership termination.
  • • Report any damage to equipment immediately.
  • • Proper footwear is required when using equipment.
  • • Be careful around mirrors to prevent accidents.

3. Cleanliness & Hygiene

  • • Wipe down machines, benches, and mats after use.
  • • Maintain personal hygiene.
  • • Dispose of bottles, wrappers, and trash in bins.
  • • Gym bags must be stored in lockers or designated areas.

4. Conduct

  • • Respect all members and staff. Harassment, verbal abuse, or intimidation is not allowed.
  • • Unsafe or disruptive behavior is not allowed.
  • • Be polite — share equipment during busy hours and help spot others when needed.
  • • The gym is not responsible for personal disputes between members.
  • • Any illegal activity or acts against Maldivian law or Shari’ah will lead to immediate termination and possible reporting to authorities.

5. Lost & Found

  • • Lost items must be claimed within 48 hours.
  • • Unclaimed valuables will be disposed of after 48 hours.
  • • The gym is not responsible for lost or stolen personal items.

6. Liability Waiver

  • • The gym and staff are not responsible for injuries, loss, or damage while using the facilities.
  • • Members train at their own risk and agree not to hold the gym responsible.

7. Payments & Membership Policy

  • • Membership billing period is from the 1st to the 30th of each month.
  • • The monthly fee is MVR 555.
  • • A one-time registration fee of MVR 45 is required when joining.
  • • Daily access costs MVR 55 per day.
  • • School student membership costs MVR 500 per month.
  • • 6-month membership offer: MVR 2,955 (registration fee not included).
  • • All fees are non-refundable.
  • • If a membership is stopped or ended, the registration fee of MVR 45 still applies if re-registering.
  • • Payments must be made before the 5th of each month.
  • • If payment is not made by the 10th, the membership will end automatically, and re-registration with the MVR 45 fee will be required to continue.
  • • Members who pay on time and follow the rules may receive annual rewards.
  • • The gym may change fees or rules, but members will be informed beforehand.

8. Termination of Membership

  • • Repeated or serious rule violations
  • • Unsafe or inappropriate behavior
  • • Late or missed payments
  • • Engaging in illegal or Shari’ah-prohibited activities

9. Online Form Submission

  • • By submitting this online form, you agree to all rules in this agreement.
  • • Members under 18 must have the form completed and submitted by a parent or legal guardian.
  • • The parent or guardian submitting the form accepts full responsibility for the underage member following all gym rules.
  • • Only registered members or authorized guardians are allowed on gym premises. No one else may enter.
